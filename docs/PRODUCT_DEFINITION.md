# Product Definition

## Product Name

Working title: **Household Cookbook**

---

# Vision

A collaborative cooking app for households that combines recipes, a shared shopping list, and simple recipe nutrition into one seamless workflow.

The app removes the friction of sharing recipes, coordinating grocery shopping, and preparing meals together.

---

# Target Users

Primary:

* Couples
* Families
* Households that cook together

Characteristics:

* Share cooking responsibilities
* Shop together or independently
* Prefer cooking from their own recipes
* Don't need advanced meal planning

---

# Problem

Current cooking workflows are fragmented.

Users typically have:

* recipes in notes, chats or photos
* shopping lists in another app
* nutrition calculations in spreadsheets

This creates duplicated work, missing ingredients and poor collaboration.

---

# Solution

Provide one shared household workspace where signed-in members can:

* store recipes
* build a collaborative shopping list from recipes
* cook using clear step-by-step instructions
* optionally calculate nutrition per serving

---

# Core Principles

1. **One household**

   * Shared data
   * Single source of truth
   * Each account belongs to one household

2. **Accounts establish access**

   * Each person signs up and logs in with their own account
   * New members create a household or join one through an invite
   * Access to recipes and the shopping list is limited to household members

3. **One shopping list**

   * Persistent
   * Never recreated
   * Recipes add items into it

4. **Recipes are the center**

   * Shopping originates from recipes
   * Cooking happens from recipes
   * Macros belong to recipes

5. **Simple first**

   * No meal planner
   * No pantry management
   * No calorie diary
   * No social features

---

# MVP Features

### Accounts and Household Access

* Sign up
* Log in and log out
* Create a household during onboarding
* Join a household through an invite
* Invite another member from household settings

### Recipes

* Create recipe
* Edit recipe
* Ingredients
* Cooking steps
* Search
* Favorites

### Shopping List

* Add ingredients from recipes
* Merge duplicate ingredients
* Manual items
* Cross off purchased items
* Real-time collaboration

### Cooking

* Ingredient checklist
* Large step-by-step instructions

---

# Post-MVP

* Nutrition per recipe
* Nutrition per serving
* Recipe import
* OCR / image scanning
* Nutrition database integration

---

# Success Criteria

A household can:

1. Sign up, then create or join its household.
2. Create a recipe.
3. Add it to the shopping list.
4. Shop together using live synchronization.
5. Cook using the app.
6. Never need to search chat history or paper recipes again.

---

# Non-Goals (MVP)

* Meal planning
* Daily macro tracking
* Diet management
* Pantry inventory
* AI recipe generation
* Public recipe sharing
* Grocery delivery integration
* Multiple households per account

---

# Product Philosophy

The app should feel like **one shared kitchen notebook**, not a complex cooking platform. Every feature should reduce friction in the natural flow of **plan → shop → cook**, while staying fast, collaborative, and easy to use.
