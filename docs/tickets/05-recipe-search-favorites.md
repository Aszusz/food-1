# 05 - Recipe Search and Household Favorites

**Type:** AFK
**Blocked by:** [04 - Recipe Create, View, and Edit](04-recipe-create-view-edit.md)

## What To Build

Add recipe discovery to the household Recipe List. Search filters recipes by title without leaving the list. Members can mark and unmark recipes as favorites. Favorites are household data, matching the information architecture's Household -> Favorites model, and are displayed in the Favorites section.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Search filters the visible recipe list by title and has a clear no-results state.
- [ ] A member can favorite and unfavorite a recipe from the recipe UI.
- [ ] Favorited recipes appear in Favorites and remain favorited after refresh.
- [ ] A second member of the same household sees the household favorite.
- [ ] Recipes and favorites from another household never appear in results.
